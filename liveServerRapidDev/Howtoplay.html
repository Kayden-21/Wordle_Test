How to play

Guess the wordle in 5 tries.

  • Each guess must be a avalid 5 letter word.
  • The colour of the tiles will change to show you how close your guess was to the word.

Examples:

A is not in the word in any spot.

  • A new wordle is released daily.
  • Once you have played the worlde of the day, there is not a second chance.
  • Score is based on the amount of time it took to guess the wordle correctly.
